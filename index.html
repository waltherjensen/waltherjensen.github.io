About me

I am a PhD Fellow in Human Computer Interaction at Aalborg University, Denmark and am currently on a 4 month study abroad in the HCI Engineering Group at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (MIT CSAIL). My research aims to ease the know-how required to manually fabricate electrochromic displays, in addition to also investigating in which application cases this display type can be used.

I have a background in Media Technology and have worked with vibrotactile feedback for navigational cues, interactive urban installations and drone - human interactions, specifically investigating when and how drones can communicate to humans.
